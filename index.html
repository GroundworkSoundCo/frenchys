A Denton Legacy: More Than Just a Business

Frenchy's Lawn & Tree Service isn't just a company; it's a commitment to the Denton community. Founded by Frenchy himself, a true local pillar, we've built our reputation on hard work, reliability, and a genuine passion for serving our neighbors. When you see our signature orange vans, you know it's a sign of quality and dedication at work.

From a small venture started in 1974 to Denton's premier lawn service, our story is one of community and care. We treat every lawn as if it were our own and every customer like family.

Deeply Rooted in the Denton Community

Our commitment goes beyond lawns and trees. Frenchy is a passionate supporter of the arts, local events, and philanthropic causes that make Denton a wonderful place to live. You'll rarely find a community event that he misses!

The "Van of Fame"

Our iconic orange vans often double as a moving tribute, parked around town to recognize the people, places, and events that define our community. It's our unique way of saying 'thank you' to Denton.
